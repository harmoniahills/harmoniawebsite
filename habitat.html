Sustainable Gated Villa Complexes

Harmonia Habitat is developing a series of ecologocally sustainable gated villa complexes.
The project in Kuchambe is currently under active development. A series of gated private communities are being developed with premium residency features that blend into the natural habitat that they are surrounded by. Each villa cluster will be green energy certified with continuous electricity and potable water provisioning. Each villa is beautifully landscaped and has the options for being customized to fit every need.
23 villas are being developed in the current phase on 3000 sq.ft. plots.
